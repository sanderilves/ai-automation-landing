Case study

Real work. Already running.

Built for a Baltic fintech — a compliant, automated debt collections workflow, production-ready and running.

The problem
  • Specialists manually checked the CRM every day to decide who to call
  • No automated tracking of whether a customer had already been contacted that day
  • Every call outcome had to be logged by hand — slow and inconsistent
  • No audit trail if a compliance question came up later
What we built
  • Automatically identifies which customers need to be contacted each day
  • Checks the right contact window before any call is made
  • Sends the call to an AI voice agent — no specialist needed for routine cases
  • Escalates to a human automatically when the case needs judgment
  • Logs every interaction for compliance without any manual effort

FAQ

Common questions.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

Any business where people are spending time on repetitive manual processes that software could handle. The problems we solve are common across many sectors. If you're unsure whether automation makes sense for your situation, book a free call and we'll tell you.

How long does it take to build an automation?

Most automations are live and running within 2–4 weeks. We start with a free discovery call to map the process, then build and test in your environment before handing over. Simple single-process automations can be delivered in under a week.

Do I need technical knowledge to use the automations?

No. We build automations that run in the background without any technical input from your team. We also provide documentation and a handover session so your team understands what's running and why.

What tools do you use?

Our core stack is n8n for workflow automation, large language models (LLMs) for AI-powered document processing and decision logic, and Python for custom integrations. We connect to the tools your business already uses — CRMs, email, Google Sheets, banking APIs, and more.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We build automations that run within your existing infrastructure where possible. We follow GDPR requirements and can provide a data processing agreement if required. We do not store your business data on third-party servers without your explicit approval.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on the complexity of the process being automated. We offer a free 30-minute discovery call to assess your situation — after that we provide a fixed-price proposal with no hidden costs. Book a call to get started.
